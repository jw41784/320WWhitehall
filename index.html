Things to Do

Mount Nittany Iconic hike with overlooks of Beaver Stadium and the valley. 5 miles round trip, moderate difficulty.
Musser Gap Gateway to Rothrock Forest. Multiple trail options from easy to challenging.
Shingletown Gap Beautiful streamside hiking with wooded loops and ridge climbs.
Colyer Lake Trail Easy 2.5-mile loop around a peaceful lake. Great for families.
Black Moshannon State Park Unique bog ecosystem with boardwalks. 25 minutes west, worth the drive.
The Arboretum at Penn State Free botanical gardens with beautiful walking paths and seasonal displays.
Downtown State College Walkable downtown with shops, restaurants, and street art.
Bellefonte Charming Victorian town with antique shops, breweries, and historic architecture.
Discovery Space Interactive science museum perfect for families with children.
Tussey Mountain Skiing in winter; concerts, festivals, and drive-in movies in summer.
Penn's Cave America's only all-water cavern tour. Also features wildlife park.
Coworking Spaces Happy Valley LaunchBox - Free community space | New Leaf Initiative - Community focused
Farmers Markets North Atherton - Saturdays, May-Nov | Downtown - Fridays, year-round

üèà Penn State Football Game Days

Game Day Alert: Downtown and campus will be extremely busy on home game days. Plan accordingly!
Tailgating Starts early morning (some as early as 6 AM). Amazing atmosphere throughout campus.
Parking Expect special event rates ($40-60). Arrive very early or use shuttles.
Shuttles CATA game-day buses - $2.50 one-way, $7 day pass. Much easier than driving!
Restaurants Book reservations weeks in advance for game weekends. Many places have special menus.
Pro Tip Even without tickets, the downtown atmosphere during games is electric. Watch at a sports bar for the full experience!
Best Sports Bars for Games Champs Downtown (139 S Allen St) - Right in the heart of downtown
Bill Pickle's Tap Room (106 S Allen St) - Penn State classic with BBQ and craft cocktails
Primanti Bros (130 Heister St) - Famous Pittsburgh-style sandwiches with fries and slaw
